In CS201, much of the course content is learned through practicing principles in small group assignments, called 'Creative Projects.' As you are well aware, these Creative Projects are accomplished in randomly-selected partnerships, unless individuals have asked to perform the work alone. These partnerships are usually announced via the 'Content' tab in BYU's LearningSuite, in a rather unappealing, generic table with numeric group assignments.


Erik and Lucien have taken the task of improving this method by creating a much more appealing and informative way to learn who your next partner will be, and get to know a little bit about them as well! Simply select your name in the drop-down menu, and get to know your partner!
